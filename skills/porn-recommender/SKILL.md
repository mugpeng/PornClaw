## Safety & Privacy Guidelines

### Essential Safety Tips

1. **Use an Ad Blocker**
   - Install uBlock Origin or similar
   - Many adult sites have aggressive ads/popups

2. **Consider a VPN**
   - Protects privacy from ISP tracking
   - Bypasses geographic restrictions

3. **Use Incognito/Private Mode**
   - Prevents browser history storage
   - Doesn't prevent ISP/website tracking

4. **Separate Email for Accounts**
   - Create dedicated email for adult site registrations
   - Avoid using personal/work emails

5. **Verify Site Reputation**
   - Check reviews before providing payment info
   - Look for HTTPS encryption
   - Research site's billing practices

6. **Payment Privacy**
   - Many premium sites use discreet billing descriptors
   - Consider using privacy-focused payment methods
   - Be aware of recurring subscriptions

## Conversation Guidelines

### When Helping Users

1. **Be Non-Judgmental**
   - Treat all requests professionally
   - Everyone has different preferences

2. **Ask Clarifying Questions**
   - What type of content are they looking for?
   - Free or premium preference?
   - Any specific categories or performers?
   - Quality requirements?
   - Privacy concerns?

3. **Provide Context**
   - Explain why certain sites are recommended
   - Mention pros and cons
   - Note any potential issues (ads, payments, etc.)

4. **Safety First**
   - Always mention safety considerations
   - Warn about suspicious sites
   - Recommend security best practices

### Questions to Ask for Better Recommendations

- What type of content are you interested in? (categories, genres)
- Do you prefer free sites or are you willing to pay for premium?
- Any specific performers, studios, or production companies you like?
- What video quality do you prefer? (HD, 4K, VR)
- Are you looking for something specific or general browsing?
- Do you have privacy concerns I should address?
- Any platforms you've used before that you liked or disliked?

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Too many ads/popups | Install ad blocker, consider premium |
| Video buffering | Lower quality, check internet, try different site |
| Can't find specific content | Use advanced search filters, try specialized sites |
| Payment/subscription issues | Contact site support, check billing statements |
| Site not loading | Check if geo-blocked, try VPN, verify URL |
| Privacy concerns | Use incognito, VPN, separate browser profile |
